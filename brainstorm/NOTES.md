## Client Types (from proposals)

### 1. Multi-System Municipal (Athabasca County)
- 5 lagoon systems across the county
- Ongoing annual treatment programs
- Complex dosing calendars (seasonal: cold/warm weather products)
- Sludge surveys, effluent quality monitoring over years
- $42,495+ annual deal
- **App needs**: Multi-site dashboard, dosing schedules, historical data tracking, sludge/effluent trending

### 2. Single-Use / System Upset (Dawson City WWTP, Yukon)
- One-time treatment for system upset
- Custom diagnosis + product recommendation
- $6,700 one-time sale
- May not become recurring app client
- **App needs**: Intake form, system diagnostics, product recommendations

### 3. Regional Collection System (SRDRWC - South Red Deer)
- 7 lift stations serving 22,431 people
- Complex odor remediation with sampling programs
- Tiered service options (A/B/C at $14K-$25K)
- Multiple sampling sites, data uploads per site
- Sites further apart geographically
- **App needs**: Map-based site management, sampling data uploads, H2S monitoring, dosing optimization

## Web App Feature Ideas (derived from proposals)

### Core Modules
1. **Client/Account Management** - Different tiers (municipal, single-use, regional)
2. **System Registry** - Track lagoons, lift stations, WWTPs per client
3. **Sampling & Monitoring** - Upload/track water quality data per site over time
4. **Treatment Plans** - Dosing schedules, product applications, calendar view
5. **Product Catalog** - Products with descriptions, pricing, application guides
6. **Proposal Generation** - Create/track proposals for prospects
7. **Performance Dashboard** - Effluent quality trending, sludge surveys, before/after comparisons
8. **Case Study Builder** - Generate case studies from client data (anonymized)

### Data Points to Track (per system)
- Effluent quality: BOD, TSS, ammonia, phosphorus, COD, pH, DO
- Sludge surveys: volume by cell, total solids %, volatile solids %, specific gravity
- H2S readings (for collection systems)
- Chemical usage (Bioxide, ACC products)
- Odor complaints log
- Treatment dosing history
- Flow rates, retention times
- Temperature data

### User Roles
- ACCWS Staff (admin, full access)
- Sales reps (proposals, client management)
- Field technicians (dosing logs, sampling uploads)
- Clients (view their data, dashboards, reports)
- Client operators (data entry, monitoring)
